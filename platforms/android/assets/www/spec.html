The competitive edge of an organisation depends upon, "beyond expectation” experiences of its Customer, when connecting with it.


While professionalism and soft skills of people attending to the customer connection are the main contributors of the experience, fulfilling the objective of the customer within the shortest possible time with quality, will be the determining factors for long lasting impression of the organisation.


This is only possible if, strategic processes that may span across internal departments, external suppliers, moving delivery services, etc are identified and executed efficiently such that they are not easily repeatable by competitors.


The strategic and tactical effectiveness and efficiency of the processes are best attained if the processes are :


  • analysed into components; by activities, tasks, process rules, business rules, product rules, workflows, KPI, etc,

  • refined according to regulatory requirements, political and legal framework, local de-facto standards, cultural behaviours, and social aspects and,

  • synthesised into a seamless process with intense focus on customer’s objective only.
